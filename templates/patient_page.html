Instructions for Doners to Donate Blood

Wait Duration: Wait for at least 56 days (or 8 weeks) between whole blood donations to ensure your body replenishes its red blood cell count. Alcohol Consumption: Refrain from consuming alcoholic beverages for at least 24 hours before donating blood to ensure accurate testing and a smooth donation process. Healthy Lifestyle: Maintain a balanced diet and hydrate well before donating blood to support your body's recovery post-donation. Medical Eligibility: Ensure you meet the minimum health requirements for donation, such as having a healthy hemoglobin level and being free of certain infections. Rest After Donation: Take it easy after donating blood. Avoid intense physical activity or heavy lifting for at least a few hours to prevent dizziness or discomfort.
